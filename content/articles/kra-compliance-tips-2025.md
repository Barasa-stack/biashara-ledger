Tax compliance is one of the biggest challenges for Kenyan business owners. With KRA increasingly digitizing its systems, staying compliant requires the right tools and knowledge.

## Key Deadlines to Remember

| Tax Type | Filing Frequency | Deadline |
|----------|-----------------|----------|
| Income Tax | Annual | June 30th |
| VAT | Monthly | 20th of following month |
| PAYE | Monthly | 9th of following month |

## Common Compliance Mistakes

### 1. Mixing Business and Personal Finances
This is the most common mistake. Always maintain a separate bank account for your business.

### 2. Missing Receipts
KRA requires you to keep records for 5 years. Digital receipt management makes this easy.

### 3. Late Filing
Penalties for late filing can add up quickly. Set reminders or use software that automates the process.
